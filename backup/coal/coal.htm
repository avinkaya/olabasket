Indonesia is the World's third largest exporter of steaming coal (in 2000, Australia 186.75 Mt, South Africa 69.91 Mt, Indonesia 55. 05 Mt), however it is also the world's leading exporter of sub-bituminious coal which represents the bulk of Indonesian coal production. In this latter area Indonesia has pioneered market acceptance of its product and will continue to expand this in the new millennium.
