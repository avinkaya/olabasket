Kavin Kayu is the manufacturer cum exporter of indoor,outdoor furniture and home furnishings. our furniture factories are located in Central Jawa cities of Jepara and Solo, Indonesia. The company is managed by expatriate professionals who are well experienced in international trade.
We have our own designers and well trained quality control personnels. Since we have in-house manufacturing facilities we make sure to give timely deliveries to our clients. We are a company that believe in manufacturing good quality furniture at competitive price and at the same time provide best services to our customers.Our products are well accepted in Australia, Europe, Africa, North and South American markets.
The main item of our production are :
 a) Antique reproduction furniture ( solid mahogany and teak wood )
 b) Rustic furniture ( mahogany )
 c) Garden furniture ( kiln and dry solid teak wood )
We are capable of offering different kind of finishes namely : melamine, nc, burn, crackel, white wash, rustic etc. We also accept to manufacture special designs from the buyers.
