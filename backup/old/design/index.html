ISMP An Overview :

Indo Swiss Medicare Products (ISMP) is a manufacturer and exporter of medical appliances to mainly Middle and South East Asia , Europe , Africa and Latin American countries. We have business associates in few countries through whom we are able to reach our local customers faster and provide efficient services. Our strength is in Surgical Instruments and Hospital Supplies.

The company was founded by an NRI and now an Indonesian citizen Mr Vinod K. Agarwal BSc, MBA, in the year 1994 with the support of medical professionals in the field of Orthopaedic Surgery. We are grateful to Mr Michael Mattsson of Neuchatel, Swiss for his special efforts and guidance in incorporating ISMP. Since then the company has spread its manufacturing base in India and Indonesia. Our inhouse manufacturing of orthopaedic Implants is located at two places in Delhi, India. The designing, Product development and Quality control sections are well equipped with modern and latest computerised technology. Besides our own manufacuring facility we have a professionally qualified team to outsource from ISO Certified and OEM Manufacturers.
